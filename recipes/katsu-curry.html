Katsu Curry

Description:

Japanese style potato curry served on rice with Schnitzel, a white cabbage & cucumber salad and takoyaki sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Japanese Curry Stock
  • Vegetable Broth
  • Potatoes (Waxy)
  • Carrots
  • Rice
  • Green Onion
  • White Cabbage
  • Cucumber
  • Dill
  • Vinegar
  • Olive Oil
  • Schnitzel
  • Takoyaki Sauce (brown sauce works too)

Steps:

  1. Peel the potatoes and cook for ~20 minutes.
  2. Chop up the vegetables and fry the carrots and white cabbage in a pan or wok for a few minutes. Leave some cabbage for the salad.
  3. Mix a glass of water with vegetable broth and add it to the pan. Let everything cook.
  4. Wash the rice and cook for ~20 minutes.
  5. Fry the Schnitzel in a separate pan or heat it up in the oven.
  6. Once the potatoes are cooked add them to the pan with the other vegetables.
  7. Add the green Onion and the Curry stock to the pan and stir until the curry is liquid. Let it cook until you are happy with the consistency.
  8. Prepare the salad while waiting for everything to finish.
  9. Once the Schnitzel is done, cut it into stripes.
  10. Put everything into a bowl and add the takoyaki sauce and green onions.
  11. Enjoy!
